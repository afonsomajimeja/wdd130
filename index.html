Republic of Mozambique, is a country located in southeast Africa.
It shares its borders with several neighboring countries:
Tanzania to the north, Malawi and Zambia to the northwest,
Zimbabwe to the west, and Eswatini and South Africa to the southwest.
The Indian Ocean lies to the east of Mozambique,
separating it from the Comoros, Mayotte, and Madagascar.
The capital and largest city of Mozambique is Maputo
